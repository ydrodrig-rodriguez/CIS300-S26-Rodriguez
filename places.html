Places I've Been To


Local

San Diego

I travel to San Diego a lot for doctor appointments(with it being a 2 hour drive). Due to this I have been able to explore the city and find some hidden gems. One of my favorite popular attractions has been the San Diego Zoo. When it comes to zoos, I can be very picky because I care deeply about the welfare of animals. That being said, I believe the San Diego Zoo is a good zoo. The animals are not forced to perform. As a result, sometimes the animals are not visible. Another place I like to go is Balboa Park. It is a great place to go for a walk and enjoy the beautiful scenery. I would sugggest this park as a budget-friendly option.

Los Angeles

Unlike San Diego, Los Angeles(LA) is a city I visit less frequently(4 hour drive away from me). However, I would still consider it a local destination. One of my favorite places to visit in LA is Disneyland. I have been to Disneyland multiple times and I have always had a great time. The rides are fun and the atmosphere is magical. Another place I like to visit is Universal Studios Hollywood. It is a great place to go for a day of fun and adventure. The rides are thrilling and the shows are entertaining.


Mexico

Los Cabos

When I visit Los Cabos, I was in middle school. However, I remember it being a beautiful scenic destination. My dad decided on doing a little over a week long road trip down Baja California during my thanksgiving break. We got to visit other cities inbetween like La Paz, Santa Rosalia(a city where my grandma lived), and more.We were also able to embark on hikes because of the roadtrip. My family and I enjoyed the beaches and the water activities. One of my favorite things to do in Los Cabos was to go snorkeling. The water was clear and warm, and I was able to see a variety of marine life. Another activity I really enjoyed was paddleboarding. I would recommend this destination to anyone looking for a relaxing beach vacation.

Cancun

I got the privilege of visiting Cancun during my spring vacation. This vacation was unlike any other I had experienced before. To begin with, it was my first time traveling through plane so that alone made it an exciting experience. I stayed in Cancun for a little over a week. The resort my family and I stayed at was amazing. The beach was absolutely beautiful. The water was so clear and blue. I am the type of person who won't go in the water if it doesn't look clean so the water in Cancun was perfect for me. During our stay at the resort, I got the chance to go parasailing. The food at the resort was also amazing. My family and I got a deal where all of our food would be covered by the resort so that was a weight taken off our shoulders. There were a variety of resort restaurants to choose from and the food was delicious. While the resort was enough to keep us entertained, we also got the chance to go on a few excursions. We did 2 excursions, Xplor and Xelha. At these excursions, I got to be along side mantarays while learning about them and also scuba diving, allowing me to see so many fish! We also got to go ziplining and see the beautiful scenery from above. Overall, it was a great vacation and I would definitely recommend it to anyone looking for a tropical getaway.

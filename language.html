Culture & Language


Culture

When visiting other countries, it is important to do research on the culture and daily customs. Understanding how to behave in a foreign country is very essential to avoid offending anyone. For example, when traveling to Japan, it is considered rude to tip at restaurants. In the United States, tipping is expected and is a way to show appreciation for good service. In Italy, dinner is typically seen as a social event especially when eating at a restuarant. In France, it is customary to greet people with a kiss on the cheek. In Mexico, it is common to greet people with a handshake or a hug. It is important to be aware of these differences to be polite and respectful.


Language

When visiting other countries it is very important to show respect by learning(at the very least) a couple basic phrases in the their language.
